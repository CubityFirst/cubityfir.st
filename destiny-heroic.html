Glimmer Extraction

Destroy 3 Glimmer Nodes

Destroy the smaller node in each extraction zone. This generally appears shortly after the extractor appears on the ground nearby. Defeating the enemies before the node will move the zone on, with new nodes.

EDZ, Nessus


Disrupt Vex Construction

Capture 3 Vex Plates

Stand on and secure the three capture plates around the event. Beams of light from the Spire show the locations of the plates.

Nessus, IO


Cabal Excavation

Destroy the Cabal Thresher

Capture 30% of the drill and destroy the Thresher Ship that flies around. It's within shooting range around 40%-45% captured.

EDZ, Nessus, Mars


Ether Resupply

Kill the Small Elite Servitors

Kill all of the smaller Elite Servitors that spawn around the Boss before they disappear.

EDZ, Nessus


Destroy the Arsenal Walker

Deactivate 3 Forcefields

Destroy a leg on the Spider Tank and use the arc charges to disable the three forcefields. (2 per/requires 6 charges)

EDZ, Titan


Injection Rig

Destroy Open Vents

Kill the Elite Psion to open vents, then proceed to destroy the three tiers of open vents on the sides of the rig.

EDZ, Nessus, Mars


Witches Ritual

Destroy 2 Shielded Crystals

Kill the two shielded Wizards by standing on the Sigil Plates. Stay on the plates to shoot/destroy

Titan, Mars


Taken Blight

Damage the Main Blight

Enter the smaller blight(s) to get the buff 'Blight Receding', which lasts for 5 sec and allows damaage to the main blight.

The Dreaming City, EDZ, IO


Defeat The Gate Lord

Destroy Vex Crystals

During the second phase, look to the back of the island and there will be a floating crystal. Destroy it. Crystals will create platforms. Repeat until you reach the top and stand on the plate.

Mercury


Defend The Warsat

Kill the 3 Shriekers

Kill the two shielded Wizards that appear in order for a Shrieker to open up. Kill it. Repeat this 3 times before reaching 100% secured.

Mars


Cryo-Pod Freeze

Shoot the Pod Vents

Once the pod begins to electrify the floor, shoot the smoking vents on the lower part of the pod, grab a cryo orb, and throw it at the boss. Repeat 3x.

The Tangle Shore


Stop The Ether Ritual

Kill Mini Boss, Shoot Blobs

Kill the Chiefton that spawns, Blobs will begin floating toward the Servitor. Shoot the blobs, preventing them from reaching the Servitor. Repeat 3x.

The Dreaming City, The Tangle Shore


Rift Generator

Destroy Blights

At the end of each wave, a Blight will spawn over the water. Destroy it. Repeat 3x and a Giant Blight will spawn over the Rift Generator surrounded by three Wizards. Kill all three Wizards before the rift generator is destroyed and you'll be teleported into the Ascendant Realm to defeat a powerful boss.

The Dreaming City
