Every now and then, in the Discord, people get rep, either for crafting, transferring profiles or similar. For a long time I've been taking rep for Buying / Selling Mathmatical Hoe Blueprints, Basket of Seeds and much much more. Others however, take reps from crafting for others, taking time out of their day to help them out by using their large number of unlocked collections to craft something quick and easy.

In other games, such as TF2, CS:GO or other games with a cash-market. Rep is used to signify risky trades / trades in which it's entirely one-sided for atleast one part of the transaction, you can see my trust page on backpack.tf for an example.

One of the *requirements* for giving out rep, is that it actually involves a "risky trade", not just spending a moment of your day helping them. If anything, doing risky trades is more than "just helping someone".
